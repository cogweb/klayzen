About Me

Klayzen (Brian) is a front-end web developer / designer with a knack for user experience and user interaction. He has been in the industry for nearly ten years and is always doing everything in his power to keep up with its' latest standards and trends. He has coffee in his blood-stream. He also doesn't like to use the third-person perspective, so from now on, it will be in first-person.

I am currently located in Cebu City, in the Philippines, but am originally from Orlando, Florida in the U.S.A. I am currently training myself to become a game designer, as video games are a lifelong passion of mine and I feel that I can contribute to the industry. My other hobbies include music, reading fantasy & sci-fi novels and traveling.
