About Me

I am a highly experienced front-end web developer with a background in graphic and web design. I have been in the industry for ten years now and am constantly evolving as a developer. I have specifically worked with distributed teams over the past few years, and have become accustomed to that environment. I have focused on WordPress development as my specialty, but I do dabble in other systems such as ExpressionEngine and Drupal. My true passion lies in pixel-perfect CSS, however, and I am always on the lookout for new techniques and methods to make my clients' web presence shine.

I am currently located in Cebu City, in the Philippines, but am originally from Orlando, Florida in the U.S.A. I am open to relocation in the future. My hobbies include coffee, late night video game marathons and sleeping on boats.
